About Shronda Jeanine

Dedicated to healing, growth, and community support

Our Mission

We provide compassionate support and resources for individuals and families affected by addiction, trauma, and homelessness. Through community-centered healing approaches, we create safe spaces for recovery, growth, and connection.

Our Approach

We believe in the power of community healing, combining traditional support methods with holistic wellness practices. Our programs focus on building resilience, fostering connections, and empowering individuals to create positive change in their lives.

Our Services

Comprehensive support for healing and recovery

Trauma Recovery Support

Compassionate guidance and resources for individuals healing from trauma, with focus on building resilience and coping strategies.

Addiction Recovery Programs

Comprehensive support systems for individuals and families navigating addiction recovery, including peer support and family counseling.

Housing & Stability Support

Resources and assistance for individuals experiencing homelessness, connecting them with housing options and stability services.

Holistic Wellness

Integrative approaches to healing including meditation, mindfulness practices, and community wellness activities.

Community Events

Community Garden Projects

Healing Meditation Circles

Wellness Workshops

Support Group Meetings

Peer support groups for individuals and families affected by addiction, trauma, and homelessness.

Monthly Community Celebrations
